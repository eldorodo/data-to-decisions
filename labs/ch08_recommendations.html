Why NDCG?
NDCG penalizes relevant items ranked lower.
A relevant item at rank 1 is worth more than at rank 10.

DCG = Σ rel_i / log₂(i+1) NDCG = DCG / IDCG where IDCG = DCG of perfect ranking.
