Optimal Solution
Simplex Intuition: The Simplex algorithm starts at a corner of the feasible region and "walks" along edges to adjacent corners, always improving the objective. It stops when no neighboring corner is better — that corner is optimal. In 2D you can see this as sliding the iso-profit line until it just touches the last vertex of the polygon.
