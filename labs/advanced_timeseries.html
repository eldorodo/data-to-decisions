Model Identification

AR(1)
ACF decays exponentially. PACF cuts off after lag 1. This is the signature of an AR(1) process.
How to read ACF/PACF:
AR(p): ACF decays (exponential/oscillating). PACF cuts off after lag p.
MA(q): ACF cuts off after lag q. PACF decays gradually.
ARMA(p,q): Both ACF and PACF decay gradually.
Bars outside the blue dashed bounds are statistically significant at the 95% level.
