Parameter Meaning

p=1 (AR): Current value depends on 1 lagged value — autoregression.

d=0 (Diff): No differencing — series assumed stationary.

q=1 (MA): Current value depends on 1 lagged error term — moving average.

What to Look For

Stationary series:
Constant mean, constant variance, ACF decays quickly
Non-stationary series:
Trend, growing variance, ACF stays high for many lags
Rolling statistics:
Rolling mean and std should be flat for stationarity
Key insight: Most ARIMA models require stationary input. Difference until the ADF test gives p < 0.05 — then d = number of differences applied.
